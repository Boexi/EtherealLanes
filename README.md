# EtherealLanes
I originally made this game as a joke in a couple hours. I have since been asked to update it with actual "real" features. It is what it is, play this game if you want to waste roughly 2 minutes of your day.

**(ALL GOLD BALLS CAN BE USED)**

## Controls:

WASD -  Walk\
Space - Jump\
E - Pick up ball\
E (ball in hand) - Drop ball\
Hold E - Charge throw\
R - Respawn Balls\
Escape - Volume menu\
Walk to  the exit door to close the game.

## New features:

Added ability to pick up ball\
Added ability to throw ball\
Replaced pins with actuals pins instead of cylinders\
There are now 5 lanes to play on\
Multiple ball spawns\
New Lighting and Materials for several things

## Known Issues:

Walking / Jumping in to walls will  stick the player in to them\
Jumping on top of the other customers\
Balls can be thrown through wall if stood next to it\
Respawning ball whilst holding will offset ball position but remain held\
Pins do not clear consistently
